Website Site Plan

Site Name

Golden Spoon Restaurant

The name "Golden Spoon Restaurant" was chosen because it represents quality dining and a warm, welcoming environment. The word "Golden" suggests excellent food and service while "Spoon" symbolizes homemade meals and comfort.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce visitors to the fictional restaurant, highlight popular meals, display the menu with prices, and provide contact information such as location and opening hours.

Scenarios

  • What meals are available and how much do they cost?
  • Where is the restaurant located and what are the opening hours?

Color Scheme

Dark Red (#8B0000) – Used for headings and navigation.

Light Cream (#FFF8F0) – Used as the background color.

Golden Accent (#D4A373) – Used for highlights and accents.

Typography

Playfair Display – Used for headings.

Poppins – Used for body text.
